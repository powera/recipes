# Classic Cabbage Salad

A super easy, and tasty, quick salad you can make for any meal!

- **Yields:** 6 servings
- **Prep Time:** 10 mins
- **Total Time:** 10 mins

---

## Ingredients

### Salad

- ½ head of green cabbage
- 4 scallions
- ½ cup sunflower seeds
- ½ cup chopped almonds
- 1 package of crushed ramen noodles

### Dressing

- ½ cup oil
- 3 tbsp vinegar
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 chicken flavored ramen noodle seasoning packet

---

## Directions

1. Chop the cabbage and scallions, and place in a large bowl with the almonds and sunflower seeds.
2. In a small container, combine all ingredients for the dressing, and set aside.
3. When you are ready to serve, pour the dressing over the cabbage, stir, and add the crushed ramen noodles.
