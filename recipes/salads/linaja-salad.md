# Linaja's Salad

A layered mason-jar salad with quinoa, chickpea salad, and fresh herbs. Makes approximately 4–6 jars.

---

## Components

### 1. Quinoa (Bottom Layer — 1/3 of jar)

#### Ingredients

- 3 cups quinoa
- 1 can (15 oz) corn, drained

#### Instructions

1. Rinse quinoa thoroughly.
2. Cook in an Instapot using the standard rice/grain setting (1:1.25 quinoa to water ratio).
3. Fluff with a fork and let cool.
4. Mix with drained corn.
5. Toss with **Quinoa Dressing** (see below).

#### Quinoa Dressing

- Juice of 1 lemon
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar (with the "mother")
- 1½ tsp cumin
- Salt — 3 shakes
- Black pepper — 5 grinds

Whisk together and toss with quinoa-corn mixture.

---

### 2. Chickpea Salad (Middle Layer — 1/3 of jar)

#### Ingredients

- 2 cups chickpeas (cooked or canned, drained)
- 1 cup corn, drained
- Garlic, to taste
- Lemon juice, to taste

#### Prepared Chickpeas (Basic)

1. Drain and rinse canned chickpeas, or use home-cooked.
2. Toss with garlic, lemon juice, and **Chickpea Salad Sauce** (see below).

#### Roasted Stovetop Chickpeas

1. Heat a dry skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add drained chickpeas in a single layer.
3. Toast, stirring occasionally, for 8–12 minutes until golden and slightly crispy.
4. Season with garlic powder, salt, and a squeeze of lemon while still warm.
5. Toss with corn and sauce.

#### Chickpea Salad Sauce

- Soy sauce — 1 part
- Lemon juice — 1 part
- Vinegar — ¼ part
- Additional spices to taste (cumin, coriander, chili pepper)

Combine and adjust seasoning to taste.

---

### 3. Fresh Herbs & Vegetables (Top Layer — 1/3 of jar)

- Red pepper, diced
- Carrot, shredded or diced
- Good cabbage (green or purple), thinly sliced

Combine and place on top of the chickpea layer.

---

## Store Recipe: Garbanzo Bean Salad

A separately prepared garbanzo bean salad that can be used as the chickpea layer or served alongside.

### Ingredients

- Garbanzo beans (1–2 cans, drained)
- Red onion, finely diced
- Fresh cilantro, chopped
- Fresh parsley, chopped
- Lemon juice, to taste
- Garlic, minced, to taste
- Soybean oil, to taste
- Coriander
- Cumin
- Chili pepper

### Instructions

1. Combine garbanzo beans, red onion, cilantro, and parsley in a bowl.
2. Whisk together lemon juice, garlic, soybean oil, coriander, cumin, and chili pepper.
3. Toss with bean mixture. Adjust seasoning to taste.

---

## Assembly: Canning / Mason Jar Instructions

Layer in order:

1. **Bottom (1/3):** Quinoa and corn mixture, dressed with quinoa dressing
2. **Middle (1/3):** Chickpea salad (prepared or roasted), dressed with chickpea sauce
3. **Top (1/3):** Fresh herbs and vegetables (red pepper, carrot, cabbage)

Seal jars and refrigerate. Best consumed within 4–5 days.

---

## Notes

- For meal prep, keep the dressings separate and add just before eating.
- Roasted chickpeas add a nice texture contrast — best added fresh rather than pre-jarred.
