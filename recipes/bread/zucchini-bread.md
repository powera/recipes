# Zucchini Bread

**Author:** Carol Pokodner Power
**Difficulty:** Intermediate
**Yields:** 12 Servings (2 loaves)
**Prep Time:** 20 mins
**Cook Time:** 1 hr
**Total Time:** 1 hr 20 mins

An easy to make treat everyone will enjoy.

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup oil
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups finely grated zucchini
- 3 tsp vanilla
- 3 cups flour
- 1½ tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1½ tsp baking soda
- 3 tsp cinnamon
- 1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
- 1 cup chocolate or peanut butter chips (optional)

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs until fluffy. Add all of the other ingredients and mix thoroughly.
2. Grease two bread pans and divide mix evenly. Bake at 325°F. Makes 2 loaves.

## Tips

- Doubling or tripling the batch works great as the loaves freeze well. This is a great way to use all your garden zucchini.
- For a variation, replace 1/3 cup of flour with 1/2 cup of cocoa for Chocolate Zucchini Bread, or add your favorite candy chips!
- For a lower calorie alternative without losing any of the flavors, substitute up to half of the oil with an equal amount of unsweetened applesauce. You can use cinnamon or regular applesauce if you prefer.
