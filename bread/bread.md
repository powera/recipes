# Bread

A simple homemade bread recipe.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 packet (2¼ tsp) active dry yeast
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 cup warm water (110°F / 43°C)

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, dissolve sugar and yeast in warm water. Let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.
2. Add olive oil and salt. Gradually mix in flour until a dough forms.
3. Knead on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm spot for 1 hour until doubled.
5. Punch down dough, shape into a loaf, and place in a greased 9×5 inch loaf pan.
6. Cover and let rise another 30–45 minutes.
7. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
8. Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped.
9. Cool on a wire rack before slicing.

## Notes

- For a crispier crust, place a pan of hot water on the oven's lower rack while baking.
- Bread keeps at room temperature for 3–4 days, or freeze for up to 3 months.
